The Challenge

Modern Housing Demands Modern Solutions

As the demand for affordable and resilient housing grows, traditional construction methods struggle to keep pace with rising costs, labor shortages, and climate threats.

Speed of Construction

Conventional methods are time-consuming and labor intensive, impacting availability and affordability of housing in high-demand areas.

Environmental Resilience

Homes are increasingly vulnerable to fire, hurricanes, earthquakes, and corrosion — demanding stronger, more durable construction solutions.

Rising Material Costs

Traditional construction relies on multiple costly materials and skilled-labor-intensive processes, making affordable housing out of reach for many communities.

Energy Efficiency

Rising energy costs and environmental concerns require construction solutions that are both energy-efficient and environmentally sustainable.

Regulatory Delays

Lengthy permitting and compliance requirements slow down project timelines, delaying critical housing projects in underserved communities.

Design Adaptability

Diverse regions and climates require housing solutions that withstand varied conditions while offering design flexibility to suit local aesthetics.

The Solution

Theis Construction Panels

Setting a new standard in modern construction. Each panel is durable, adaptable, and affordable — built to withstand extreme weather, fire, and corrosion, while achieving the appearance of any desired exterior finish.

Featuring Tekton foam construction for adaptable R-values, the panels maximize energy efficiency and reduce project costs. SIP assembly shortens construction time and expense, with flexible options for pre-fitted HVAC, plumbing, and electrical.

Structural Support

Replaces traditional wall and roof framing with a fully integrated, load-bearing structure — eliminating multiple installation steps.

Tekton Foam Insulation

Adjustable R-values to suit any climate. Airtight insulation reduces heating and cooling costs, enhancing comfort and structural integrity.

Zero VOC & Eco-Friendly

Minimal waste production and zero VOC emissions reduce environmental impact on every job site.

How It Works

Rapid SIP-Style Assembly

Panels can be pre-manufactured or fabricated directly on-site — reducing labor, installation time, and cost. No waiting period.

1

Panel Fabrication

Pre-manufactured off-site or fabricated directly on the job site — both options with no lead time penalty.

2

Structural Assembly

Panels lock together forming the full structural skeleton — replacing framing, sheathing, weather barrier, and roof in one product.

3

MEP Integration

Optional pre-fitted HVAC, plumbing, and electrical channels embedded during fabrication, reducing on-site trade coordination.

4

Finish & Complete

Exterior finishes tailored to any style. Interior panels complete the build using standard or Theis Interior Panels. Move-in ready fast.
