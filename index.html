The Challenge

Construction Today Is Broken

The industry faces critical pain points that demand a new approach to building — one that is faster, cleaner, and more adaptable.

Speed Demands

Traditional construction timelines can't keep pace with the urgent demand for affordable housing, post-disaster rebuilding, and rapid community deployment.

Environmental Resilience

Conventional materials fail in extreme weather events — flooding, hurricanes, freeze-thaw cycles. Communities need structures that truly endure.

Rising Material Costs

Supply chain disruptions and inflation have made standard lumber, concrete, and steel prohibitively expensive — squeezing margins and delaying projects.

Energy Inefficiency

Poorly insulated conventional construction drives up long-term operational costs and carbon footprint — failing occupants and the planet simultaneously.

Regulatory Delays

Unproven building systems face lengthy approval cycles. Theis Tekton panels are engineered to meet and exceed existing code requirements out of the box.

Design Limitations

Most rapid-build solutions sacrifice aesthetics for speed. Tekton panels accept unlimited finish options — stucco, wood cladding, brick veneer, and more.

Rapid Assembly

From Foundation to Framed in Days

The Tekton system is engineered for speed. Small crews, minimal equipment, and a simple four-step process replace weeks of traditional framing.

01

Panel Delivery & Staging

Panels arrive pre-cut to spec. No on-site cutting required — just stage and sequence by floor plan.

02

Foundation Connection

Anchor panels to the foundation sill plate using the Tekton clip system — no special tools needed.

03

Panel-to-Panel Joining

Interlocking spline joints create a continuous air and moisture barrier as panels are stood and fastened.

04

Roof & MEP Rough-In

Channels for electrical and plumbing are pre-routed. Mechanical rough-in proceeds immediately after panel erection.

50% faster than conventional wood-frame construction on comparable projects
